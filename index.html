About Me

My name is Nour Kahouach, I am 23 years old and I recently started learning web development. Initially, I studied fundamental English at ISEAHG. It was always a pleasure for me to read all types of literature. Besides reading the books I love and discovering new ones, I was also able to study linguistics and The various sets of rules that govern how language works and the mechanisms by which it operates. Studying English was fun but as much as I loved studying it, I didn't want to pursue it neither in an academic nor a professional setting. I quickly lost interest in the theoretical aspect of it all. I wanted to learn something more practical . Hence why , I chose web development . at first it didnt seem as the right choice for me since i have no background in computer science , but i love a challenge . There's no better way than hands-on experience and that's what GOMYCODE has taught me . And this is what kept me going . I can say today that it has been one of the best decisions in my life . You don’t have to have a computer science degree to be a web developer . There are tons of resources online that can help you learn the basics . From there it’s all about practice . One of my favorite ways to learn is through Youtube tutorials there's a plethora of content that you can access for free.
